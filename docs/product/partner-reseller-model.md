# Partner and Reseller Model

## Goal

Support partners managing multiple customer workspaces without collapsing tenant isolation.

## Model

- Partner accounts manage delegated access into customer workspaces.
- Delegation is explicit, scoped, and revocable.
- Cross-tenant operations require strict audit logging.

## Future capabilities

- Portfolio dashboard across delegated tenants.
- Aggregated billing and entitlement views.
- Partner-level support tooling with policy gates.

# Forbidden Shortcuts

- Do not bypass `PermissionEvaluator`.
- Do not use raw role checks everywhere.
- Do not use raw JSON blobs where typed domain entities are required.
- Do not put provider logic inside domain services.
- Do not build many services too early.
- Do not add large files that mix concerns.
- Do not skip tests.
- Do not skip audits.
- Do not query analytics from OLTP tables at scale.
